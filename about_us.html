About Our Charity

Roots of love foundation Uganda is an youth Non Government Organization was founded in 2022 after a birthday charity of one of our co-founders in july but after seeing the need in the community we organized our self from anonymous visitors to working for pleasure to putting a smile onto the faces of people in the slum area with the ghetto youths on issues of HIV Aids drug abuse and Menstrual health in its early stages and also those with less privileges for scholastic materials to attend school.

Roots of love foundation Uganda has since spread its wings to the other parts of the country and have engaged the youth more especially in post primary and secondary school students on pertinent issues in the districts of Kampala, Wakiso, Jinja and Namutumba.

About Us

Roots of Love Foundation Uganda, a youth Non-Government Organization, was established in 2022 following a birthday charity event. Their mission is to address critical community needs, including HIV/AIDS awareness, drug abuse prevention, and menstrual health education. They also support underprivileged students by providing scholastic materials. The organization has expanded its reach to other parts of the country, actively engaging post-primary and secondary school students in districts like Kampala, Wakiso, Jinja and Namutumba.
